Welcome! You will shortly be placed into a virtual maze.
The goal of this task is to learn a route in the maze, by following the red arrows on the floor, that leads you to find the exit.
You will be tested on your knowledge of this route later. Please let the experimenter know if you have any questions.
To begin, press the blue "Continue" button with the trackpad, and walk towards a set of footprints on the floor.
